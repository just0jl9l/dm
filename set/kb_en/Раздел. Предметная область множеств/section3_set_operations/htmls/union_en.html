Formal definition for the union of two sets
A U B = { x | x ∈ A ∨ x ∈ B}

Further examples

  • {1, 2, 3} ∪ {3, 4, 5} = {1, 2, 3, 4, 5}
  • {а, б} ∪ {3, 4} = {а, б, 3, 4}
  • {1, 2} ∪ ∅ = {1, 2}

    Properties of the union operation
  • А ∪ ∅ = А - Identity law
  • А ∪ U = A - Domination law
  • A ∪ A = А - Idempotent law
  • А ∪ B = B ∪ А - Commutative law
  • А ∪ (B ∪ C) = (А ∪ B) ∪ C - Associative law.
